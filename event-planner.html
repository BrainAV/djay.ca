Built for the Modern DJ Business

📋 The Problem We're Solving

Running a DJ business means juggling dozens of tools — spreadsheets for clients, email threads for contracts, sticky notes for song requests. DJ & Event Planner Pro consolidates everything into a single, purpose-built platform designed the way DJs actually think and work.

🎯 Who It's For

Solo DJs, small DJ companies, and event entertainment agencies who want to appear more professional, reduce administrative overhead, and focus on what they do best — delivering incredible performances and unforgettable experiences.

Planned Features

👥 Client Management

A full CRM tailored for the DJ industry. Track every lead, client, and past event. Store preferences, notes, and communication history in one place — no more digging through inboxes.

📅 Event Calendar

Visual scheduling with conflict detection. See all upcoming gigs at a glance, manage availability, and share professional calendar links with clients for easy booking.

🎵 Music Request Portal

Give each client their own personal portal to submit song requests and "do not play" lists ahead of the event. Eliminate last-minute request chaos and deliver a curated experience.

📄 Contracts & Invoicing

Generate professional, legally-sound contracts with e-signature support. Invoice clients directly and accept payments without stepping outside the platform.

📱 Mobile-First Design

Manage your business from anywhere. Fully responsive and optimized for phones and tablets — because your office is wherever the gig takes you.

🤖 AI Assistance (Future)

Intelligent playlist suggestions based on client preferences, automated follow-up emails, and lead scoring to help you focus your time on the gigs that matter most.
