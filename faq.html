Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is DJ MixKit?

DJ MixKit is a browser-based DJ software that allows users to mix tracks without any installation. It offers advanced features for professional DJs and is accessible from any device.

2. Do I need to download anything to use DJ MixKit?

No, DJ MixKit runs entirely in your browser. There is no need for downloads or installations, making it easy to use on any device.

3. Can I use DJ MixKit offline?

Yes, DJ MixKit can be used offline once it is loaded in your browser. You can mix tracks without an internet connection.

4. What types of audio files can I use with DJ MixKit?

DJ MixKit supports a variety of audio file formats, including MP3, WAV, and more. You can drag and drop your audio files directly onto the decks.

5. Is there a mobile version of DJ MixKit?

Yes, DJ MixKit is designed to be responsive and works well on mobile devices. You can mix tracks on your smartphone or tablet.

You can reach out to us through the contact page on our website. We are here to help with any inquiries or support you may need.
