General FAQ

More specific product FAQs can be found directly on the DJ MixKit and Radio Stream Player pages.

What is DJAY.ca?

DJAY.ca is a standalone sub-brand born out of the Brain AV organization. We specialize in building innovative, browser-based audio tools, streaming internet radio, and providing professional DJ booking services across Canada.

Are your web applications free to use?

Yes. Currently, tools like DJ MixKit and the Radio Stream Player are free and open-source. They run entirely in your local browser using powerful web APIs, meaning we don't have heavy server costs to pass on to you.

Do I need an account to save my settings?

Currently, no account is required. In the future, we plan to implement user accounts so you can seamlessly synchronize your saved radio streams and DJ configurations across devices.

How do I book a DJ for my event?

We provide professional services for weddings, corporate events, and private parties. Please visit our Booking page to explore availability with Jason Brain or our curated nationwide network.

How can I get technical support?

If you encounter bugs in our software, the best place to report them is on our respective GitHub repositories. For general inquiries, please use our contact form.
