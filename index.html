How It Works

  1. Load Your Music: Drag and drop your audio files onto a deck, or use the browse button.
  2. Mix and Scratch: Use the intuitive controls to mix, sync, and manipulate your tracks.
  3. Create Your Sound: Add effects, adjust tempo, and use the crossfader to create seamless transitions.
  4. Save Your Session: Export your mix and settings to continue later.

About DJ Mixkit Pro

DJ Mixkit Pro was created by a team of passionate developers and DJs who believe that everyone should have access to high-quality mixing tools. Our mission is to provide a powerful, accessible, and fun DJing experience right in your web browser.

This is the first release of our web app, and we're excited to see what you create with it. For more information, contact us at info@djay.ca.
